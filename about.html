About Us
    History of Our Hospital
Established in 1952, Our Hospital has served as a vital component of the local healthcare system for more than 70 years. We began as a modest clinic and swiftly grew, adopting cutting-edge surgical procedures and novel medical practices in the 1960s and 1970s. We were well-known for providing outstanding maternity and pediatric care by the 1980s. We still lead in several disciplines today, providing all of our patients with cutting edge care and compassion. For many years, we have been a dependable source of healthcare due to our unwavering dedication to providing high-quality care.
    Vinzeres hospital Canada

Our Canadian branch, established in 1990, serves as the flagship location for our hospital network. Situated in the heart of Toronto, this state-of-the-art facility has become a beacon of medical excellence in Canada. Known for its advanced technology , comprehensive patient care, and top-tier medical professionals, the Canadian branch offers a wide range of specialized services, from cutting-edge surgical procedures to groundbreaking research in oncology and cardiology. With a strong commitment to innovation and community health, our Canadian hospital continues to set the standard for quality care and medical advancements in the region.

The most sophisticated and opulent of all the Vinzeres hospitals, our Dubai branch opened its doors in 2015.
