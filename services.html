Services

Our mission at Vinzeres Hospital is to provide top-notch medical care. We have locations in Dubai, India, and Canada. Every patient will receive the best care possible thanks to the expertise of our team of qualified medical professionals. Some of the main services we provide are as follows:

  • Emergency and Trauma Care: Around-the-clock, emergency medical care for injuries, accidents, and serious illnesses.
  • General Surgery: State-of-the-art technology is used by skilled surgeons to provide comprehensive surgical therapy for a variety of illnesses.
  • Diagnostic imaging: cutting edge facilities for MRI, CT, ultrasound, and X-rays to provide prompt and precise diagnosis.
  • Maternity & Childcare: Full range of services for expectant mothers and their infants, including pediatrics, postpartum care, and prenatal exams.
  • Cardiology: Comprehensive heart care services, encompassing interventional, diagnostic, and prophylactic cardiology procedures.
  • Orthopedic Services: Expert Medical Attention
